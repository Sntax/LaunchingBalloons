Interested in Launching Your Own Balloon? Here's what you need to know.

Launching balloons can be a very exciting and fun expeirence however it can also be very dangurous if the proper precautions are not taken prior to launch. The information below is meant as an aid and guide however we strongly suggest that you go over proper handling techniques for compressed gas as well as talk in depth with an FAA official about the risks involved. Below I have outlined some primary saftey tips before getting started.

Safety

  1. Determine your launch date and contact the FAA regarding the launch. This is not normally required for smaller balloon launches but it is definitely strongly recommended. They will lay out your local laws about launching balloons like this as well.
  2. Find a local gas supplier, examples below and read their safety information about Hydrogen and the risks associated with its usage. Hydrogen is extremely flammable and it is very important to know how to handle it properly.
  3. Try to avoid launching alone. Get a group of friends together, this is a fun project and it's better to have a few hands in the event of a problem. Having lots of friends contribute it gets everyone involved and makes it much more manageable.
