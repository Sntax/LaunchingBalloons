Interested in seeing Earth from space?

We are a group of friends dedicated to photographing our blue world from the bottom of a weather balloon.

We hope to highlight the pros and cons of launching weather balloons and filming at high altitudes in an attempt to give back to the scientific community with our efforts.

With each launch we add to our technology and improve our process. Here is how we do it.

We started with a lot of trial and error and extensive research. As with any scientific pursuit, there are very serious risks involved with sending a weather balloon into space. We implore you to brush up on our recommended safety tips before persuing this venture any further. We are in no way, shape or form liable or responsible for any injury, damage to property or incidents that may occur as a result of this information. Remember to have fun out there but always proceed with caution and at your own risk. Good Luck!
