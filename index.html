We are a group of friends dedicated to photographing earth from space while hitching a ride on the bottom of a weather balloon. We hope to highlight the pros and cons to these endevours while attempting to give back to the scientific community with our efforts.

With each launch we add to our technology and improve our process. Here is how we do it.

What we do is done by trial and error and extensive research. As with any scientific persuit, there are very serious risks involved with sending a weather balloon into space. We implore you to brush up on our reccommended safety tips before persuing this venture any further. We are in no way, shape or form liable or responsible for any injury, damage to property or incidents that may occur as a result of this information. Proceed at your own risk! Have fun out there.

We are an expanding group of collegues who continues to push each consecutive launch one step further. Meet the team.

We strive to improve on our designs and try new technologies in search of the best results possible with the least amount of risk.
